Hello. I'm Julie V. Scher. I'm a Board Certified Behavior Analyst®.

I have been a BCBA® since May 2017.

For over 10 years, I have worked with children and adults living with autism, and other developmental challenges.

I recieved my Master of Arts degree in Applied Behavior Analysis from Ball State Univeristy in 2015. While at Ball State, I earned the Dean's Citation for Academic Excellence in recognition of a perfect academic record. In 2009, I obtained an undergraduate Specialized Honours Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology from York University.
